About Us

National Manpower Solutions

National Manpower Solutions is an Australian owned company, which was established with the view of providing reliable and professional:
  • Security;
  • Cleaning;
  • Maintenance;
  • Facilities and Property Management;
  • Hospitality and Events;
  • Traffic Management; and
  • Construction

packages to the commercial, retail and domestic sectors designed to meet each client’s individual needs. Our company employs experienced, qualified, reliable and dedicated personnel in all levels of its Management. National Manpower Solutions is proud to boast a reputation of consistency, professionalism and high-level practices that have resulted in achieving success through the establishment of client satisfaction and loyalty.

National Manpower Solutions has remained competitive throughout the years and have sustained a reputation of contract longevity, professionalism, accountability and best practice methodologies by offering unparalleled ‘delivery of service’ to the industry.

We endeavour to provide an efficient service to our customers, through offering committed, ethical, efficient and cost effective service. We constantly add value in our services by upgrading our skills, which in turn strengthen our brand reliability as well as enhance our customer satisfaction levels. We are proud to deliver highly competitive rates without compromising best practice standards from their highly trained personnel.

National Manpower Solutions is a proud member of the Australian Security Industry Association (ASIAL), The National Body for the Australian Security Industry and is pre-qualified for CM3 (Contractor Safety Management).
